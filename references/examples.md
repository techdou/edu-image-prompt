# Educational Prompt Examples

## 1. Concept Analogy: Token (Lego Bricks)
- **Input**: What is a Token?
- **Output**:
### Token: AI 积木 | Analogy
- **Recommended Reason**: Abstract concepts are best explained through tactile analogies.
- **On-Image Copy**:
  - 主标题：Token：AI 的积木
  - 副标题：理解 AI 如何“阅读”和“思考”
  - 重点项：文字拆成积木 / 每一块都要钱 / 空间有限防超载
  - 金句：像搭积木一样理解 AI
- **The Prompt**: A 16:9 horizontal 3D educational illustration about "Tokens". A sentence is being decomposed into colorful, translucent Lego-like bricks, each labeled with binary codes (0,1) or short syllables. Visual layout: Text flowing from left to right, turning into neatly stacked bricks on a clean laboratory desk. Text rendered in clean Chinese: Title "Token：AI 的积木", Subtitle "像搭积木一样理解 AI". Style: bright, minimalist, pastel colors, white background. High clarity, educational infographic style. No garbled text, no messy lines, no dark atmosphere.

# Prompt And Copy Rules

## Educational Goal
Create visual materials that help students understand complex concepts instantly.
- **Priority**: One clear message per image.
- **Tone**: Professional, friendly, and encouraging.
- **Clarity**: High contrast between text and background.

## The "Standard Formula" for Copy
- **Main Title**: Strong, action-oriented or concept-naming.
- **Subtitle**: A one-line bridge between the metaphor and the tech.
- **Key Points**: Sparse, punchy fragments. Adapt the number to the visual type (e.g., 2 for comparison, 3 for columns, 4-6 for maps).
- **Gold Sentence**: A memorable takeaway.

## Visual Style: Modern Education Technology
- **Canvas**: Default **16:9** (Aspect ratio), but adapt to user request (e.g., 9:16 for posters).
- **Background**: Pure white (#FFFFFF) or very light gray (#F8F9FA).
- **Accents**: Professional blues, soft greens, or friendly oranges.
- **Hierarchy**: Use size and weight to distinguish the title from the points.
- **Whitespace**: Keep at least 40% of the image as empty space to avoid clutter.

## Prompt Requirements
- **Direct Output**: The generated prompt should be a single paragraph.
- **Mandatory Keywords**: "clean", "bright", "modern education technology", "high whitespace".
- **Mandatory Negatives**: "garbled text", "dense small text", "complex background", "cyberpunk", "neon glow", "commercial advertisement aesthetic".

## Copy Limits
- Main title: Max 12 Chinese characters.
- Subtitle: Max 24 Chinese characters.
- Points: Max 8 Chinese characters each.
- Total text: Stay under 60 characters for maximum readability.
